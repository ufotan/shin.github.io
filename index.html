Computational Linguistics

Computational linguistics is a not so well known field in the world of linguistics. In this field, linguists are given a few known translations of an unknown language. Then, linguists need to try to find out the structure and grammatical rules of the langauge, and translate the other unknown sentences. It's like a puzzle-solving game which requires a creative mind and strong analytical skills. I absolutely fell in love with computational linguistics when I first learned about it. It's like decoding a secret language, and each discovery feels like a small victory. It has equipped me with invaluable skills in breaking down complex questions and thinking out of the box.
